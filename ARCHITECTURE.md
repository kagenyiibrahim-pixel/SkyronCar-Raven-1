# Raven-1 System Architecture Overview

## 1. Power Reactor Feedback Loop
The core innovation of the Raven-1 is the closed-loop energy recovery system. By coupling high-torque axial flux motors with synchronized dynamos, we aim to minimize external charging requirements.

## 2. Aura-OS Control Layer
Aura-OS acts as the nervous system, managing:
- Real-time voltage balancing.
- Kinetic energy capture optimization.
- Thermal management of the 800V bus.

## 3. Future Roadmap
- Implementation of AI-driven predictive power distribution.
- Expansion of the Raven-1 platform into industrial power storage solutions.
